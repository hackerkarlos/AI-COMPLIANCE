# GDPR Requirements Research

## Overview
This document outlines the key GDPR compliance requirements for Danish SMBs (Small and Medium-sized Businesses) to inform the EUComply assessment system.

## Risk Factors

### 1. Data Processing Volume
- **Low Risk**: Basic contact information and business records
- **Medium Risk**: Employee data, customer preferences, marketing lists
- **High Risk**: Special category data (health, ethnic origin, political opinions)

### 2. Data Subject Categories
- **Low Risk**: Business contacts, suppliers
- **Medium Risk**: Employees, customers
- **High Risk**: Children, vulnerable individuals

### 3. Geographic Scope
- **Low Risk**: Denmark only
- **Medium Risk**: EU-wide operations
- **High Risk**: International transfers outside EU/EEA

### 4. Technology Infrastructure
- **Low Risk**: Basic office software, local storage
- **Medium Risk**: Cloud services, CRM systems
- **High Risk**: AI/ML processing, IoT devices, complex integrations

## Regulatory Requirements

### Core GDPR Articles
- **Article 6**: Lawful basis for processing
- **Article 7**: Conditions for consent
- **Article 13-14**: Information to be provided
- **Article 15-22**: Data subject rights
- **Article 25**: Data protection by design and by default
- **Article 30**: Records of processing activities
- **Article 32**: Security of processing
- **Article 33-34**: Personal data breach notification
- **Article 35**: Data protection impact assessment
- **Article 37**: Designation of data protection officer

### Danish Specific Requirements
- **Danish Data Protection Act**: National implementation
- **Datatilsynet**: Danish Data Protection Authority requirements
- **Industry-specific regulations**: Finance, healthcare, etc.

## Compliance Assessment Framework

### Risk Scoring
- **High Risk (76-100 points)**: Requires immediate attention, likely DPO required
- **Medium Risk (51-75 points)**: Significant compliance gaps to address
- **Low Risk (26-50 points)**: Basic compliance measures needed
- **Minimal Risk (0-25 points)**: Limited GDPR obligations

### Question Weights
- Special category data processing: 15 points
- International transfers: 12 points
- High volume processing (>10k subjects): 10 points
- No formal processes for rights: 8 points
- No security measures: 8 points
- No incident response: 6 points
- Cloud storage without safeguards: 5 points
- No staff training: 4 points
